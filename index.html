EXPLORE THE BEST EXERCISES

Cycling

Plan to ride your bike for 30 to 60 minutes. Every ride should begin with a warm-up. Initially pedal at a modest, easy speed. Then accelerate enough that you begin to burn calories.

Walking

Just 30 minutes of daily walking can strengthen bones, improve cardiovascular fitness, lower excess body fat, and build muscle strength and endurance.

Swimming

The finest workout might be swimming. Your body is supported by the buoyancy of the water, which also relieves pressure from aching joints so you may move more freely.

Yoga

Yoga is a spiritual practise that focuses on balancing the mind and body. It’s based on a complex science. Living a healthy lifestyle is both an art and a science.
