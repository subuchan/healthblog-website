About Us.

Our Mission

The board-certified physicians and respected health and wellness professionals on the Verywell Fit Review Board review our articles and join us in our quest to inspire everyday action with practical, science-backed nutrition and fitness advice for men and women.

A Few Words About Us

Healthghuru is a nonprofit organisation dedicated to getting people moving across the world by certifying health coaches and exercise professionals. We collaborate with like-minded organisations, top research institutions, federal and state policymakers, fitness industry leaders, and diverse community organisations to increase physical activity for improved public health.


We invest in our aim to get people moving by providing NCCA-accredited certification programmes, continuing education, and easy-to-understand evidence-based health and fitness information to people of all backgrounds.

Our Aim

By 2035, we want to be the leading nonprofit advocate for achieving health through physical activity and other lifestyle changes by providing NCCA-accredited certifications and scientifically rooted education to fitness professionals and health enthusiasts, with the goal of having a significant impact on preventable, lifestyle diseases related to inactivity.
